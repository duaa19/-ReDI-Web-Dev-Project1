who we are

Our motto is "Knowledge is the key to civilization."

* We are an educational center with numerous teachers located throughout Germany.
* We connect teachers with students by allowing parents to search our website by location and age group.
* We cover all grades from first to twelfth, plus vocational training.
* Online classes available worldwide in English or German, and live sessions across Germany.
* Qualified teachers, fair prices, and flexible scheduling.
